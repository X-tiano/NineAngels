What we host

Three things, done well.

Weddings

Ceremony under the rose arch, dinner on the lawn, dancing by the pool. We've watched a hundred guests fall under the same spell - the gate closes, the night opens.

Corporate Gatherings

Off-sites, summer parties, intimate launches. A walled garden setting that gets people out of meeting rooms and into actual conversation.

Private Parties

Birthdays, anniversaries, baptisms, baby showers. The kind of party where the host gets to actually enjoy themselves.
