Gallery

A walk around the villa.

Photographs by section - the way you'd actually move through the property on the day.

01 · Arrival

The Entrance

An arch heavy with climbing roses - the first photograph of every wedding, and the threshold between Madliena and the world inside.

02 · Threshold

The Gate

Hand-forged iron, taller than it looks in photos. When it closes behind you, the city outside simply stops.

03 · Centrepiece

The Lagoon Pool

Curved stone, a flat shallow shelf, and palm shadow on the water. Lit softly through the evening - never disco, always candle.

04 · Open ground

The Lawn

Flat, generous, and quietly the most useful square of grass on the island. Ceremonies in front, dinner in the middle, dancing at the back.

05 · Service

The Outdoor Kitchen and Dining

Where caterers actually want to work. Stone counters, full grill, prep space and a dining table that seats sixteen without anyone reaching.

06 · After dark

The Villa at Night

Festoon lights between the palms, lanterns on the lawn, and the pool a soft pale-blue against the dark. The hours nobody wants to leave.
