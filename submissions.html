Activating real email delivery

Right now, submissions are stored locally in this browser only — fine for testing. To forward every enquiry to info@christianopropertymanagement.com and auto-reply to the sender, do this once:

  1. Sign up at formspree.io (free plan is plenty for low volume).
  2. Create a new form pointing to info@christianopropertymanagement.com. Copy the form ID (looks like mvolwabq).
  3. Open quote.js and paste it into CONFIG.formspreeId.
  4. In Formspree → form settings → Autoresponse, paste the thank-you email body shown at the bottom of quote.js (it promises a reply within 24 hours).
  5. Formspree's dashboard then becomes your permanent, multi-device submissions log — this admin page stays as a local mirror.
