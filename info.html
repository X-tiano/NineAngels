Through July and August the villa runs exclusively as a private retreat. Event bookings resume from 1 September.

01

Booking & Payment

Confirming your date

A 50% deposit secures your date. We hold dates informally for 5 working days while you decide; after that they release back to general availability.

Final balance

The remaining 50% is due no later than 7 days before your event or check-in date. We'll send a reminder.

Damage deposit

A refundable €500 damage deposit is held by Revolut by 10:00 on the event day, and returned by midday the day after - provided the property is left in the condition it was found.

02

Cancellations

Plans change. Here's exactly what happens if yours do.

30+ days

Refund minus €150

Full deposit returned, less a €150 admin fee.

30 - 8 days

Deposit forfeited

The 50% deposit is non-refundable. No further charges.

Under 7 days

Full balance due

The full event fee is payable, regardless of cancellation reason.

03

House Rules

A handful of common-sense conditions to keep the property - and your event - safe and beautiful.

~

Pool

No lifeguard on duty - guests swim at their own risk. No glass within 2m of the water.

★

Catering

BYO caterer or use one of our trusted partners. The outdoor kitchen is yours for the day.

◆

Alcohol

Alcohol may be served but not sold on the premises. No cash bar.

♪

Music

DJs welcome with prior approval. All music off by 23:00 in line with local regulations.

✿

Decor

Freestanding decor only - nothing nailed, screwed or adhered to walls, trees or stone.

●

Smoking

Outdoors only. Ashtrays provided in designated areas; please use them.

▲

Parking

Limited on-site parking. No tall vehicles under the rose arch - height-restricted.

✕

Prohibited

No confetti, no smoke bombs, no flares, no fireworks, no illegal activities.
